Automated Materials Handling (AMH): Return + Sorting

Bibliotheca’s AMH technology represents a big shift in library operations. By automating endless tasks related to the handling of returned materials, AMH significantly reduce staff workload, allowing them to focus on more valuable tasks.

Through sophisticated sorting algorithms, advanced robotics, and transportation system, this solution has the capability to span across multiple floors and buildings to process returned materials and make it back to the correct location.

In other words, AMH is simplifying the workflow for both staff and patrons.
