Hi!

I’m Adson, a Product Marketing Specialist based in Toronto, specialized in managing product marketing campaigns. For the past 9 years, I’ve enjoyed executing and managing B2B product marketing campaigns with clients, prospecting new projects, and collaborating on the development content assets focused on customer acquisition and brand awareness.

As an experienced professional in the field, I understand that companies struggle to develop a cohesive marketing strategy that aligns with their business goals. Also, companies often find it challenging to build and maintain strong relationships with clients.

Whether you are just starting to build your brand or are an established business, as a marketing account manager, I can help you create and implement strategic plans that ensure all marketing efforts are effective. I implement tracking and analytics to measure campaign effectiveness and adjust strategies accordingly. Additionally, I focus on client engagement, feedback collection, and satisfaction, which leads to better retention and loyalty.
